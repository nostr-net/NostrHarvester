nostr.net search

Examples: bitcoin kind:1 limit:5 tag:e:some_event_id -"bitcoin" -kind:1 -"bitcoin" -kind:1
Supported filters: pubkey:<hex_or_npub>, relay:<url>, kind:<number>, since:<timestamp>, until:<timestamp>, tag:<key>:<value>, limit:<number>, offset:<number>, free text.
Prefix - to any filter or term to exclude matching events.
